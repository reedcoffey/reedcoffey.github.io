About Me

I'm a queer, trans artist and horticuluralist from Arizona, with a degree in Ecology and Evolutionary Biology and a lifelong passion for making art. My work is heavility influenced by my love of plants and the wild places I've gotten to recreate and work in. I've lived in and been inspired by the desert for the last 20 years, but my childhood in the Pacific Northwest still plays a huge role in how I approach painting- I've always loved a lush, dark green.

When I'm not painting, you can find me hanging out with my cat, working in the garden, or tinkering with my website.
