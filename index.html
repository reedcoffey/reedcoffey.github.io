Reed Coffey is a visual artist based in Tucson, Arizona. Their paintings layer brightness over realism to create colorful scenes that evoke the quiet joy of discovery- rounding the corner of a trail to a breathtaking view, lifting a leaf to reveal a cluster of berries, or the visual rhythms present for those who take the time to observe them. Rooted in their childhood in the Pacific Northwest and shaped by a career in conservation, Reed’s oil paintings capture the beauty of the landscapes and plants of the western United States. Their paintings are for those who cherish the outdoors and for those who have had a hand in protecting them.
